# Privacy Policy — Workaholic

Last updated: April 2026

## Data Collection
Workaholic does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
All processing occurs locally in your browser.

## Data Storage
Your profile settings, filters, and job history are stored exclusively
in Chrome's local extension storage on your device.
No data is sent to any external server.

## Third-Party Services
Workaholic accesses the following platforms solely to display job listings:
Upwork, Workana, 99Freelas, LinkedIn, Indeed and Gupy.
No user data is shared with these or any other third parties.

## Changes
If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be
published at this URL.
